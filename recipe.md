Soak three cups of finely chopped fenugreek leaves in hot water for 10 min. Remove the water from fenugreek leaves and keep it aside.

Then put green peas in warm water for 5 minutes and remove the water from the peas and keep it aside.

Take 100g of cashew nut and 2 medium size onions.

Cut the onions into small pieces.
 
Boil the onions and cashew nuts with 3 green chillies for 5 miutes. Once it cools down make a fine paste. Add 1/2 teaspoon green cardomonn powder to the paste.
  
Now take 1 tablespoon of Ghee, to that add 1 teaspoon ginger garlic paste and saute it for 3 minutes. Then add the onion and cashew paste. 

Then saute it to 4 minutes, or till it leaves the oil from sides. Then, add boiled fenugreek leaves and peas to it. Stir fry for 2-3 minute sand then add 1 tablespoon Amul cream. Add salt as per taste.

 Then add half cup water(100ml). Stir it, cover it and let it cook for 5 minutes. Serve it with naan.
